<p><p>The 5G wireless base station market consists of four main types: Macro BS, Micro BS, Pico BS, and Femto BS. Macro BS are large base stations that provide coverage over a wide area. Micro BS are smaller base stations that cover smaller geographical areas. Pico BS are even smaller and provide coverage for indoor spaces. Femto BS are the smallest and are typically used for personal or residential use. Each type of base station caters to different coverage needs and environments within the 5G network.</p></p>
