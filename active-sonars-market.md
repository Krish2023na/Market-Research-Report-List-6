<p><p>Active sonars are essential for underwater exploration and mapping, categorized primarily into Multi-Beam and Side Scan Sonar Systems. The Multi-Beam Sonar System efficiently captures detailed 3D images of ocean floors, making it vital for hydrographic surveys and underwater construction. In contrast, Side Scan Sonar Systems provide high-resolution images of the seafloor, ideal for locating shipwrecks, underwater structures, and marine life. Both systems cater to industries like marine research, oil and gas exploration, and environmental monitoring, enhancing underwater visibility and data collection.</p></p>
