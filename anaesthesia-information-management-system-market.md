<p><p>The Anaesthesia Information Management System (AIMS) market can be categorized into two frameworks: B/S (Browser/Server) and C/S (Client/Server). The B/S framework relies on web-based technologies, allowing users to access data from any device with internet connectivity, facilitating remote monitoring and easy updates. In contrast, the C/S framework operates on local servers, providing direct connections to workstations, which can offer enhanced security and faster data processing. Both frameworks serve to improve patient safety and streamline workflow in anaesthesia management.</p></p>
