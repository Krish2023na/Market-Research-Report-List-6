<p><p>Aramid fibers are a class of heat-resistant and strong synthetic fibers, primarily categorized into two types: para-aramid and meta-aramid. Para-aramid fibers are known for their exceptional strength and resistance to abrasion, making them ideal for applications in aerospace, automotive, and personal protective equipment. In contrast, meta-aramid fibers offer high thermal stability and chemical resistance, commonly used in insulation and fire-resistant clothing. Both types cater to diverse markets, each serving specific performance demands across industries.</p></p>
