<p><p>The Automatic Fare Collection (AFC) machine market comprises three main types: Ticket Vending Machines (TVMs), Ticket Office Machines (TOMs), and Fare Gates. TVMs enable passengers to purchase tickets autonomously, while TOMs offer additional services like refunds and information through staffed or automated interfaces. Fare Gates control access to transit systems, ensuring only paid passengers enter. Together, these components streamline the ticketing process, enhance operational efficiency, and improve the overall passenger experience in public transportation systems.</p></p>
