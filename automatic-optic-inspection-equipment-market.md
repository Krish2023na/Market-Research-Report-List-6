<p><strong>The Automatic Optic Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3D</li><li>2D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment is categorized into two main types: 2D and 3D systems. </p><p>2D AOI focuses on surface inspection, capturing images to identify defects like misalignments or soldering issues in electronic components. It uses cameras to analyze patterns and features.</p><p>3D AOI, on the other hand, adds depth perception, enabling a more comprehensive evaluation by measuring the height and volume of components. This allows for accurate detection of defects like voids or insufficient connections, enhancing quality control in manufacturing.</p></p>
