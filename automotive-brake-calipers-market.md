<p><p>Automotive brake calipers are categorized into two main types: floating brake calipers and fixed brake calipers. Floating brake calipers are designed to move laterally on sliding pins, allowing for more even distribution of pressure on the brake pads. On the other hand, fixed brake calipers remain stationary and provide more consistent braking performance. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different preferences and requirements, making them popular choices in the automotive brake calipers market.</p></p>
