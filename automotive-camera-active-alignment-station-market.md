<p><p>The Automotive Camera Active Alignment Station market is categorized by Degrees of Freedom (DOF), which define the movement capabilities of the alignment system. A 4 DOF station allows for positional adjustments in four directions, typically accommodating basic alignment needs. A 5 DOF station adds an additional rotational axis, enhancing precision. The 6 DOF station includes all five axes plus a second rotational capability, enabling comprehensive alignment for advanced camera systems, crucial for features like autonomous driving and advanced safety functionalities.</p></p>
