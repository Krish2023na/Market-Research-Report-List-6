<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive current sensor market is divided into two main types: open loop and closed loop sensors. Open loop sensors measure current without feedback, providing simpler and cost-effective solutions for applications like basic load monitoring. In contrast, closed loop sensors utilize feedback mechanisms, offering higher accuracy and improved performance, making them suitable for complex applications such as electric power steering and battery management systems. Both types serve critical functions in optimizing vehicle performance and enhancing safety features.</p></p>
