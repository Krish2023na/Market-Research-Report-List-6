<p><strong>The Automotive Electro-hydraulic Power Steering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EHPS</li><li>P-EHPS</li><li>R-EHPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electro-hydraulic power steering (EHPS) market is segmented into three main types: C-EHPS, P-EHPS, and R-EHPS. C-EHPS (Column Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering) integrates the motor and hydraulic components within the steering column, providing compact design and improved efficiency. P-EHPS (Pump Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering) uses a separate pump to generate hydraulic pressure, allowing for better customization and performance. R-EHPS (Rack Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering) integrates the system within the steering rack, offering enhanced response and precision in steering control. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements and performance goals.</p></p>
