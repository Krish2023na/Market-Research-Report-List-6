<p><ul><li>Absorbing Solar Control Glass</li><li>Reflective Solar Control Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Solar Control Glass market includes two main types: Absorbing Solar Control Glass and Reflective Solar Control Glass. Absorbing Solar Control Glass absorbs a significant amount of solar radiation, preventing it from entering the vehicle. On the other hand, Reflective Solar Control Glass reflects a portion of the solar radiation, reducing heat transmission and glare. Both types help in maintaining a comfortable interior temperature, reducing the need for excessive air conditioning and improving overall energy efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
