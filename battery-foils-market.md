<p><p>The battery foils market consists of two primary types: aluminum foil and copper foil. Aluminum foil is widely used in lithium-ion batteries due to its lightweight properties, excellent conductivity, and resistance to corrosion, making it ideal for anode current collectors. On the other hand, copper foil is essential for cathode current collectors, providing superior electrical conductivity and thermal stability. Both materials play crucial roles in enhancing battery performance, efficiency, and overall energy storage capabilities in various applications, including electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
