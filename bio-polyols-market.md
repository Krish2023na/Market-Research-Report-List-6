<p><p>Bio polyols are derived from renewable resources and are used as raw materials in the production of polyurethane foams. There are two main types of bio polyols - polyether polyols and polyester polyols. Polyether polyols are typically made from propylene oxide or ethylene oxide and are known for their flexibility and resilience, while polyester polyols are made from natural oils or acids and provide excellent strength and durability. Both types of bio polyols are increasingly being used in various industries due to their sustainable and environmentally friendly properties.</p></p>
