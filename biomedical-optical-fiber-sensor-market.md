<p><p>The biomedical optical fiber sensor market comprises various types, including fiber optic temperature sensors and fiber optic pressure sensors. Fiber optic temperature sensors utilize light transmission properties to accurately measure temperature changes within biological systems, making them essential in medical diagnostics and monitoring. Fiber optic pressure sensors, on the other hand, detect pressure variations in fluids or tissues, providing valuable data for assessing patient conditions. Both sensor types leverage the advantages of fiber optics, such as high sensitivity, immunity to electromagnetic interference, and compactness.</p></p>
