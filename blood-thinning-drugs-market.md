<p><strong>The Blood-thinning Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anticoagulants</li><li>Antiplatelet Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood-thinning drugs market consists of two main types: anticoagulants and antiplatelet drugs. Anticoagulants, such as warfarin and direct oral anticoagulants, prevent blood clots by inhibiting specific factors in the coagulation process. They are commonly used to treat conditions like atrial fibrillation and deep vein thrombosis. Antiplatelet drugs, like aspirin and clopidogrel, work by preventing platelets from aggregating, thus reducing clot formation. These medications are primarily used to prevent heart attacks and strokes, enhancing cardiovascular health.</p></p>
