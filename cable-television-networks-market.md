<p><p>Cable television networks encompass various market types, including Cable and Other Pay Television Services, which deliver subscription-based programming through cable infrastructure. Direct Broadcast Satellite Services (DBS) provide access via satellite signals directly to consumers. Closed Circuit Television Circuits cater primarily to security and surveillance needs. Satellite Master Antenna Systems (SMATV) aggregate signals for distribution in specific buildings or complexes. Multipoint Distribution System Services (MDS) utilize microwave signals for transmitting video content, while Subscription Channel Services offer on-demand programming for a fee.</p></p>
