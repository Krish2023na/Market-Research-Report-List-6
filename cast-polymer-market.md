<p><p>The cast polymer market comprises three primary types: solid surface, cultured marble, and engineered stone. Solid surface is a non-porous material known for its versatility and seamless appearance, often used in countertops and sinks. Cultured marble combines resin with natural stone, offering a more affordable and lighter alternative with a marble-like finish, popular in bathroom fixtures. Engineered stone, made from crushed stone and resin, mimics natural stone's aesthetics while providing increased durability and reduced maintenance, ideal for various applications.</p></p>
