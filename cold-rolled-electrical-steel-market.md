<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Rolled Electrical Steel market is categorized into two main types: oriented and non-oriented electrical steel. Oriented electrical steel is specifically designed to enhance magnetic properties in one direction, making it ideal for applications like transformers and electric motors, where efficiency is critical. Non-oriented electrical steel, on the other hand, exhibits uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making it suitable for various applications, such as smaller motors and generators, where flexibility in design is essential.</p></p>
