<p><strong>The Colorectal Cancer Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FOB Tests</li><li>CTC Tests</li><li>CEA Tests</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colorectal cancer diagnostics market encompasses various testing methods to detect and monitor the disease. FOB (Fecal Occult Blood) tests are non-invasive screenings that detect hidden blood in stool, indicating potential cancer. CTC (Circulating Tumor Cells) tests analyze blood samples for cancerous cells, providing insights into metastasis. CEA (Carcinoembryonic Antigen) tests measure specific protein levels in the blood, helping assess treatment response and recurrence risk. Other diagnostic methods may include imaging techniques and genetic testing, enhancing overall detection and management of colorectal cancer.</p></p>
