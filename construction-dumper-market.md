<p><p>The construction dumper market is categorized into three main types: articulated dump trucks, rigid dump trucks, and others. Articulated dump trucks feature a flexible hinge between the cab and the dump box, allowing for enhanced maneuverability on rough terrain. Rigid dump trucks, in contrast, have a fixed frame design, offering superior load capacity and stability on solid surfaces. The 'others' category encompasses specialized dump trucks and variations designed for unique operational needs, catering to diverse construction applications.</p></p>
