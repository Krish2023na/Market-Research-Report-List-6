<p><ul><li>Utomatic Transfer Switch</li><li>Static Transfer Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center transfer switches and switchgears are critical components for ensuring uninterrupted power supply. The market is primarily divided into two types: Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) and Static Transfer Switches (STS). ATS automatically transfers power from the primary source to a backup source during an outage, enhancing reliability. In contrast, STS enables seamless switching between multiple power sources within milliseconds, eliminating downtime. Both types are essential for maintaining operational continuity and protecting sensitive equipment in data center environments.</p></p>
