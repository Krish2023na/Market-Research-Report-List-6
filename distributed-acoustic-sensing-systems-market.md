<p><ul><li>DASI Type</li><li>DASP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) systems primarily fall into two categories: DASI (Distributed Acoustic Sensing Interrogation) and DASP (Distributed Acoustic Sensing Processing). DASI involves the fundamental technology that interrogates fiber optic cables to gather acoustic data, serving various applications such as monitoring infrastructure and environmental conditions. DASP focuses on the analysis and interpretation of the collected acoustic signals, enabling enhanced data processing for specific applications like security or industrial monitoring. Both types are essential for optimizing the effectiveness of DAS systems.</p></p>
