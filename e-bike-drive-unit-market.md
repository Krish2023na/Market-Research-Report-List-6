<p><p>Mid-drive motors are located in the center of the bike frame and provide a more natural riding experience by leveraging the bike's gears for efficient power transfer. They are ideal for hilly terrain and off-road riding. In contrast, hub motors are built into the wheel hub and provide a simpler and more cost-effective solution.
