<p><p>The Electroless Copper market for multilayered boards primarily includes two types: Horizontal and Vertical Electroless Copper. Horizontal Electroless Copper involves a process where boards are processed in a horizontal orientation, enabling efficient chemical distribution and uniform deposition across the surface. Conversely, Vertical Electroless Copper operates in a vertical alignment, allowing for better control in deep vias and complex geometries. Both methods are essential for ensuring reliable electrical connections in multilayer printed circuit boards, catering to various industry demands.</p></p>
