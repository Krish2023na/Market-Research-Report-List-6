MOSFET dosimeters leverage metal-oxide-semiconductor technology to offer high sensitivity and accuracy for personal radiation monitoring. The “others” category encompasses various emerging technologies, such as thermoluminescent dosimeters and electronic dosemeters, which contribute to specialized applications in radiation safety. Each type caters to specific requirements in monitoring and measuring individual radiation exposure.</p></p>
