<p><ul><li>DLP (Digital Light Processing) Pico Projector</li><li>LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Pico Projector</li><li>Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) Pico Projector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Pico Projectors market is divided into three main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) Pico Projectors, LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Pico Projectors, and Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) Pico Projectors. DLP Pico Projectors use a digital micromirror device to reflect light onto a screen, LCoS Pico Projectors use liquid crystals to modulate light, and LBS Pico Projectors use lasers to steer light onto a screen. Each type has its own unique advantages and is used in different applications based on their specific capabilities.</p></p>
