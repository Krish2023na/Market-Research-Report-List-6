<p><p>EMG monitoring system is a medical device used to measure and record the electrical activity produced by muscles. It is commonly used in the diagnosis of various neurological disorders, such as ALS, carpal tunnel syndrome, and peripheral nerve injuries. The system consists of electrodes that are attached to the skin over the muscles being tested, and a machine that amplifies and displays the electrical signals.</p><p>The EMG monitoring system market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of neurological disorders, rising geriatric population, and advancements in technology. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.80% during the forecast period.
