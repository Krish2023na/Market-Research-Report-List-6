<p><ul><li>Three Layers Engineered Wooded Flooring</li><li>Multilayer Engineered Wooded Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered wooden flooring market features two primary types: three-layer engineered wooden flooring and multilayer engineered wooden flooring. Three-layer engineered flooring consists of a solid wood top layer, a middle layer of plywood, and a backing layer, offering stability and aesthetic appeal. Multilayer engineered flooring, on the other hand, includes multiple layers of wood and other materials, enhancing durability and moisture resistance. Both types provide a practical alternative to solid wood flooring, combining beauty with efficient performance in various environments.</p></p>
