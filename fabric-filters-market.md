<p><p>Fabric filters are air pollution control devices that remove particulate from industrial exhaust streams. The two main types of fabric filters are Pulse Jet and Reverse Air/Gas filters. Pulse Jet filters use compressed air pulses to clean the filter bags, while Reverse Air/Gas filters use reverse airflow to dislodge and remove the collected particles. Both types are commonly used in industries such as cement, power generation, and metal processing to meet regulatory emissions standards and improve air quality.</p></p>
