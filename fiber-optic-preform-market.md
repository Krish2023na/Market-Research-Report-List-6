<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Preform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VAD</li><li>OVD</li><li>PCVD</li><li>MCVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optic Preform market types include VAD (Vapor Axial Deposition), OVD (Outside Vapor Deposition), PCVD (Plasma Chemical Vapor Deposition), and MCVD (Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition). VAD involves using high temperatures to deposit glass on a rotating substrate. OVD uses a chemical reaction to deposit glass on a rod. PCVD uses plasma to create thin layers of glass on a substrate. MCVD involves injecting gases into a tube to deposit glass. Each method has its own advantages and is used for different applications in the fiber optic industry.</p></p>
