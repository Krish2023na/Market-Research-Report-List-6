<p><p>The film capacitor market is primarily categorized into AC and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed for alternating current applications, providing efficient energy storage and smooth operation in devices such as motors, power supplies, and lighting systems. In contrast, DC film capacitors are optimized for direct current applications, commonly found in power electronics, DC filtering, and energy storage systems. Both types leverage polymer films as dielectrics, ensuring high stability, low losses, and excellent performance across various voltage ratings.</p></p>
