<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Dry Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Preaction Fire Sprinklers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire sprinkler market is segmented into different types based on their functionality. Wet fire sprinklers are filled with water and automatically disperse it when triggered by heat. Dry fire sprinklers are filled with pressurized air or nitrogen and release water when the heat causes the sprinkler head to burst. Deluge fire sprinklers release a flood of water when activated by a smoke or heat detector. Preaction fire sprinklers combine elements of both dry and deluge systems, requiring a detection system to trigger the release of water.</p></p>
