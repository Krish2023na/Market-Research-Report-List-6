<p><p>The fluoropolymer film market is segmented into various types based on the specific fluoropolymer used. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) offers high thermal resistance and low friction. PVF (Polyvinyl Fluoride) is known for its durability and weather resistance. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) provides excellent chemical resistance. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) combines the properties of both PTFE and FEP, while PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) is highly flexible and heat-resistant. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is valued for its transparency and toughness. Other types include lesser-known fluoropolymer variations.</p></p>
