<p><p>The galvanometer scanner market is segmented into single-axis, dual-axis, and tri-axis types, each serving different applications. Single-axis scanners move in one direction, ideal for simple tasks like laser beam steering. Dual-axis scanners offer increased versatility by allowing movement in two perpendicular directions, suitable for more complex scanning tasks like imaging. Tri-axis scanners enhance functionality further by enabling movement across three dimensions, catering to advanced applications in fields such as 3D printing, laser machining, and medical imaging.</p></p>
