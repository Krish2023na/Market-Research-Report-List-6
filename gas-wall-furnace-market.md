<p><strong>The Gas Wall Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Noncondensing Type</li><li>Condensing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas wall furnace market is segmented into two primary types: non-condensing and condensing types. Non-condensing gas wall furnaces operate at higher temperatures, expelling combustion gases without utilizing their heat, making them less efficient but often more affordable. In contrast, condensing gas wall furnaces capture and reuse waste heat, offering higher energy efficiency by condensing steam created during combustion. This results in lower emissions and reduced energy costs, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers and energy-efficient building standards.</p></p>
