<p><strong>The Gauze Wound Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Gauze</li><li>Nonwoven Gauze</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gauze wound dressing market is divided into two primary types: woven gauze and nonwoven gauze. Woven gauze is made from interlaced fibers, providing a strong, absorbent material ideal for various wound types, aiding in fluid management and allowing for better airflow. In contrast, nonwoven gauze is crafted from bonded fibers, offering enhanced softness and lower linting, making it suitable for sensitive areas and minimizing irritation. Both types serve essential functions in wound care, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
