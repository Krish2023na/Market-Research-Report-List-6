<p><strong>The Geotextiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Woven</li><li>Woven</li><li>Knitted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotextiles market is segmented into three primary types: non-woven, woven, and knitted. Non-woven geotextiles comprise layers of fiber bonded together through mechanical, thermal, or chemical processes, offering excellent drainage and filtration properties. Woven geotextiles feature interlaced fibers, providing strength and durability, often used for erosion control and reinforcement. Knitted geotextiles are produced by interlooping yarns, allowing for flexibility and elasticity, making them suitable for applications requiring adaptation to movement or load changes in geotechnical projects.</p></p>
