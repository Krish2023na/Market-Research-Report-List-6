<p><p>The GPS-equipped cycle computers market features three main types: wired computers, wireless computers, and wireless GPS computers. Wired computers connect directly to the bike's frame, offering reliable performance but limited to specific mounting positions. Wireless computers eliminate cables for easier installation and cleaner aesthetics, using radio signals to transmit data. Wireless GPS computers combine GPS functionality with wireless technology, providing advanced features like navigation and real-time tracking, appealing to cyclists seeking detailed route information and performance metrics during their rides.</p></p>
