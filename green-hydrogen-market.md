<p><p>Green Hydrogen market is segmented into three types of electrolyzers: Alkaline (ALK) Electrolyzer, Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzer, and Solid Oxide Electrolyzer. ALK Electrolyzers use alkaline solution as the electrolyte, while PEM Electrolyzers use a polymer membrane. Solid Oxide Electrolyzers operate at high temperatures and are suitable for large-scale industrial applications. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, and the choice of electrolyzer depends on factors such as cost, efficiency, and application requirements in the growing green hydrogen market.</p></p>
