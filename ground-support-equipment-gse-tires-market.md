<p><p>The Ground Support Equipment (GSE) tires market includes three main types: solid rubber, foam-filled, and air-filled tires. Solid rubber tires are durable and resistant to punctures, making them ideal for heavy-duty usage in harsh environments. Foam-filled tires combine the benefits of solid rubber with added cushioning, providing improved shock absorption and stability. Air-filled tires offer a lightweight and flexible option, enhancing ride comfort and handling but are more susceptible to punctures. Each type serves specific operational needs in ground support applications.</p></p>
