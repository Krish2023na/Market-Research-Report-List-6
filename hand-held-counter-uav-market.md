<p><strong>The Hand-held Counter UAV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Recognition</li><li>Active Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand-held counter UAV market features two primary types: passive recognition and active recognition. Passive recognition involves the detection of drones through non-intrusive methods, such as analyzing radio frequency emissions or visual identification, without alerting the UAV operator. In contrast, active recognition utilizes emitters or sensors that actively engage with the UAV, often to jam signals or disrupt flight control. Both methods play crucial roles in enhancing security measures against unauthorized drone activities in various sectors.</p></p>
