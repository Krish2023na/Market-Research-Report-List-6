<p><strong>The Handheld Slit Lamp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Handheld Slit Lamp</li><li>Binocular Handheld Slit Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld slit lamp market consists of two main types: monocular and binocular handheld slit lamps. Monocular handheld slit lamps feature a single eyepiece, offering portability and ease of use for quick examinations. They are often favored for their compact design, making them suitable for fieldwork and remote areas. Binocular handheld slit lamps, on the other hand, come with two eyepieces, providing enhanced depth perception and a more comprehensive view of the eye, making them ideal for detailed assessments in clinical settings.</p></p>
