<p><p>The hearing care devices market consists of various types of hearing aids, including Behind-the-Ear (BTE) Aids, In-the-Ear Aids, Receiver-in-the-Ear (RITE) Aids, and Canal Hearing Aids. BTE aids are placed behind the ear and are suitable for all degrees of hearing loss. In-the-Ear aids are custom-fit for the individual's ear canal. RITE aids separate the receiver from the main body of the hearing aid for better sound quality. Canal hearing aids are discreet and fit inside the ear canal for a more natural look.</p></p>
