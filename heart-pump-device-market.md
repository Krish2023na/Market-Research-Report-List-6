<p><strong>The Heart Pump Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs)</li><li>Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs)</li><li>Total Artificial Heart (TAH)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart pump device market consists of various types of devices used to support and assist the heart in pumping blood. Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are implantable pumps that help the heart pump blood to the rest of the body. Intra-aortic Balloon Pumps (IABPs) are temporary devices inserted into the aorta to improve blood flow. Total Artificial Heart (TAH) devices are fully implanted artificial hearts that completely replace the function of the natural heart. These devices cater to different needs and conditions of patients with heart failure.</p></p>
