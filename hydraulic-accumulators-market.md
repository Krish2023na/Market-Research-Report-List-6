<p><p>Hydraulic accumulators are devices used to store hydraulic energy in the form of fluid under pressure. There are three common types of hydraulic accumulators: bladder, piston, and diaphragm. Bladder accumulators use a flexible bladder to separate gas and fluid, piston accumulators use a moving piston to store fluid, and diaphragm accumulators use a flexible diaphragm to separate gas and fluid. Each type has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for different industries and hydraulic systems.</p></p>
