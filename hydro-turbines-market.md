<p><p>The hydro turbine market comprises several types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile, ideal for medium head applications, featuring a mixed flow design. Kaplan turbines are suited for low head, high flow situations, utilizing adjustable blades for efficiency. Pelton turbines excel in high head scenarios, using a jet of water to drive the wheel. Other types encompass variations like bulb and crossflow turbines, catering to specific site conditions and energy generation requirements, enhancing overall hydroelectric efficiency.</p></p>
