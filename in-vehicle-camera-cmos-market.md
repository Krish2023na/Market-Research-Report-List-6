<p><p>The in-vehicle camera CMOS market features two primary types: parallel and serial. Parallel type cameras transmit data simultaneously through multiple channels, enabling faster image processing and real-time performance, making them suitable for applications requiring high-speed operations. In contrast, serial type cameras transmit data sequentially through a single channel. While they may offer lower data transfer rates, serial cameras are often simpler and more cost-effective, making them ideal for basic applications such as rear-view systems and monitoring.</p></p>
