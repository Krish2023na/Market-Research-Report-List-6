<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial turbine mill market is categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal mills. Vertical turbine mills orient the grinding chamber vertically, allowing for efficient material flow and enhanced product consistency. They are often preferred for producing fine powders and can handle viscous materials. In contrast, horizontal turbine mills feature a horizontal grinding chamber, which provides a larger surface area for processing and can accommodate larger feed sizes. Both types serve various industries in manufacturing and processing applications.</p></p>
