<p><ul><li>Rearward-facing baby seat</li><li>Combination seat (Rearward and Forward-facing)</li><li>Forward-facing child seat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant safety seat market includes several types, each designed for specific age and weight categories. Rearward-facing baby seats provide optimal protection for newborns and infants by cradling their heads and necks during impact. Combination seats serve dual purposes, allowing for both rearward-facing use for younger children and forward-facing use as they grow. Forward-facing child seats are designed for older toddlers and preschoolers, featuring harnesses for safety while allowing more room for movement as the child develops.</p></p>
