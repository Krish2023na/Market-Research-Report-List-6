<p><p>The infrared (IR) sensor market encompasses various types, primarily thermal infrared (TIR) sensors and quantum infrared (QIR) sensors. TIR sensors detect heat emitted by objects, making them ideal for applications like temperature measurement and motion detection. In contrast, QIR sensors utilize quantum effects to detect specific wavelengths of infrared radiation, offering high sensitivity for applications in gas detection and spectroscopy. Both sensor types are crucial in fields such as automotive, healthcare, and security, driving advancements in technology and expanding their market reach.</p></p>
