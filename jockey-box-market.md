<p><strong>The Jockey Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coil Cooler</li><li>Cold-plate Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jockey box market types primarily consist of coil coolers and cold-plate coolers, both designed for serving draft beverages. Coil coolers utilize a coiled stainless-steel tubing system to circulate chilled water, effectively cooling the beer as it flows through. In contrast, cold-plate coolers feature an insulated plate with embedded cooling lines, allowing for efficient heat transfer. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal serving temperatures in outdoor events, festivals, and bars, ensuring a refreshing experience for consumers.</p></p>
