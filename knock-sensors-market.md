<p><p>The knock sensors market is primarily segmented into piezoelectric sensors and acoustic sensors. Piezoelectric sensors detect vibrations caused by engine knocking through the piezoelectric effect, making them highly sensitive and widely used in automotive applications. Acoustic sensors, on the other hand, utilize sound waves to identify knock events by analyzing noise patterns in the engine. Both types serve critical roles in engine management systems, enhancing performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions control in modern vehicles.</p></p>
