<p><p>The Label Release Liner market is primarily divided into two types: paper-based and film-based liners. Paper-based liners are typically made from cellulose fibers and offer good printability and compatibility with various adhesive types, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Film-based liners, on the other hand, are crafted from materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, providing superior moisture resistance and durability. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring smooth label application and optimum adhesion, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
