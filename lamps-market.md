HID lamps, typically used in commercial and industrial applications, offer high intensity and brightness but consume more energy. Halogen lamps, a type of incandescent lighting, provide excellent color rendering and are commonly found in residential settings but have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs. Each type serves distinct purposes based on efficiency, brightness, and application.</p></p>
