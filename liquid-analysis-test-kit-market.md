<p><p>The liquid analysis test kit market can be categorized into three types: physical, chemical, and biological. Physical test kits measure physical properties such as pH, turbidity, and conductivity of a liquid sample. Chemical test kits detect the presence and concentration of specific chemical compounds like heavy metals or pollutants. Biological test kits identify the presence of living organisms or indicators of microbial contamination in liquids. Each type of test kit serves a specific purpose in analyzing different aspects of liquid samples for various industries and applications.</p></p>
