<p><strong>The Low Methoxyl Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional(LMC)</li><li>Amidated（LMA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Methoxyl Pectin market is divided into two main types: Conventional Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMC) and Amidated Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMA). LMC is derived from citrus fruits and requires sugar or acid to gel, making it suitable for high-sugar products. In contrast, LMA is modified to accept higher levels of calcium and can gell in low-sugar or sugar-free applications, making it ideal for health-conscious products. Both types serve distinct roles in the food industry, especially in jams, jellies, and dairy products.</p></p>
