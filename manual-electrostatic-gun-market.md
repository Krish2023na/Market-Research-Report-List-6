<p><p>The manual electrostatic gun market is categorized into two main types: wired and wireless electrostatic guns. Wired electrostatic guns typically require a direct connection to a power source, providing consistent charge flow and reliability during operation. In contrast, wireless electrostatic guns offer more flexibility and portability, utilizing batteries or rechargeable power sources for operation, making them suitable for difficult-to-reach areas. Both types are used in various applications, including painting, coating, and dust removal, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in industrial processes.</p></p>
