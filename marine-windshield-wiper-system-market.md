Single Pendulum Systems feature a single wiper arm that moves in an arc, suitable for smaller vessels with limited windshield areas. In contrast, Dual Pendulum Systems utilize two wiper arms that can operate independently, providing enhanced coverage for larger windshields and improving visibility in adverse weather conditions. Both systems are crucial for ensuring safety and clear visibility on marine vessels.</p></p>
