<p><ul><li>Laser Marking Machinery</li><li>Mechanical Marking Machinery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marking machinery market can be categorized into two main types: Laser Marking Machinery and Mechanical Marking Machinery. Laser marking machinery uses laser beams to create marks on various materials with precision and speed, making it ideal for industries requiring high-quality and durable markings. On the other hand, Mechanical marking machinery uses physical mechanisms like stamping or engraving to create markings, offering a more cost-effective solution for simpler marking requirements. Both types of machinery play crucial roles in industrial manufacturing processes for product identification and traceability.</p></p>
