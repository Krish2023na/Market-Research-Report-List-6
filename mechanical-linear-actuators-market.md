The market includes various types such as screw actuators, cam actuators, and wheel and axle actuators. </p><p>Screw actuators use a threaded rod to achieve linear motion, while cam actuators use a rotating cam to move a follower in a linear direction. Wheel and axle actuators use a wheel and axle mechanism to convert rotational motion into linear movement. These types of actuators cater to different industries and applications based on their specific design and capabilities.</p></p>
