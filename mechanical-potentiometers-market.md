<p><p>The mechanical potentiometers market can be categorized into two main types: through-hole potentiometers and surface mount device (SMD) potentiometers. Through-hole potentiometers feature leads that insert into holes on a circuit board, offering robust connections suitable for various applications. In contrast, SMD potentiometers are designed for direct mounting on the surface of circuit boards, enabling compact designs and automated assembly processes. Both types play crucial roles in adjusting voltage and resistance in electronic circuits, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
