<p><ul><li>TTL Loupes</li><li>Flip-up Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Binocular Loupe market is primarily categorized into two types: TTL (Through-The-Lens) Loupes and Flip-up Loupes. TTL Loupes are integrated directly into the lenses of safety glasses, offering a lightweight and compact design for enhanced comfort and stability during extended use. In contrast, Flip-up Loupes attach to the glasses and can be flipped up when not in use, providing versatility and ease of transition between magnified and unaided vision. Both types are crucial for precision in surgical and dental procedures.</p></p>
