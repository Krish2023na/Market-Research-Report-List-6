<p><p>The medical bronchoscopy market is divided into two primary types: rigid bronchoscopes and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are sturdy, tubular instruments used for direct visualization of the airways and are primarily employed in surgical procedures, offering excellent suction and manipulation capabilities. Flexible bronchoscopes, on the other hand, are more versatile and commonly used for diagnostic purposes, allowing for easier navigation through the airway's curves. Both types play crucial roles in respiratory diagnostics and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
