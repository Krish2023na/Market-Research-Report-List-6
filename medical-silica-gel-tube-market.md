<p><p>The Medical Silica Gel Tube market primarily involves two manufacturing methods: extrusion and mold vulcanization. The extrusion method involves forcing a molten polymer through a shaped die to create continuous tubes, offering uniform thickness and flexibility, ideal for medical applications. In contrast, mold vulcanization involves shaping the silica gel in a mold and applying heat to cure it, resulting in highly precise dimensions and enhanced mechanical properties. Both methods cater to different requirements in the medical industry, ensuring diverse product offerings.</p></p>
