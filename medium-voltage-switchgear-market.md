<p><p>The medium voltage switchgear market primarily includes Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS) and Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS). AIS employs air as the insulating medium and is widely used in various applications due to its cost-effectiveness and ease of maintenance. Conversely, GIS utilizes a gas, typically sulfur hexafluoride (SF6), for insulation, allowing for a more compact design and enhanced reliability in space-constrained environments. Both types play crucial roles in electrical distribution systems, ensuring safe and efficient operation.</p></p>
