<p><p>Mescal is categorized into different market types based on aging processes. Mescal Joven, or young mescal, is unaged, capturing the pure agave flavor and often showcasing a vibrant, earthy profile. Mescal Reposado is aged for a minimum of two months, allowing it to develop a smoother, more complex taste with hints of wood and subtle smoke. Conversely, Mescal Añejo is aged for at least one year, further enhancing its richness and depth, appealing to those seeking a more refined sipping experience.</p></p>
