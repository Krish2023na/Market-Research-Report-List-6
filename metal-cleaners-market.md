<p><p>The metal cleaners market consists primarily of alkaline and acid cleaners, each serving distinct purposes. Alkaline cleaners, typically containing sodium hydroxide or other bases, effectively remove organic contaminants like oils and greases from metal surfaces. They are often used in industrial settings for maintenance and preparation. Conversely, acid cleaners utilize acids such as phosphoric or citric acid to dissolve mineral deposits, rust, or oxidation. These cleaners are crucial for surface preparation and corrosion removal, ensuring optimal metal performance and longevity.</p></p>
