<p><ul><li>ECM (Electret Capacitance Microphone)</li><li>MEMS Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Microphone market primarily includes two key types: ECM (Electret Capacitor Microphones) and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones. ECMs, known for their wide frequency response and high sensitivity, utilize an electret material to maintain a static charge, making them ideal for various applications like consumer electronics and telecommunications. MEMS microphones, on the other hand, are miniaturized sensors that leverage mechanical vibrations to capture sound, offering compact size, durability, and power efficiency, suitable for smartphones, wearables, and IoT devices.</p></p>
