<p><p>The microarray scanner systems market encompasses several types, each tailored for specific detection methods. Fluorescence microarray scanners utilize fluorescent dyes to visualize hybridized samples, offering high sensitivity and multiplexing capabilities. Charge-coupled devices (CCD) microarray scanners capture light emitted from samples, providing accurate imaging and quantification. Colorimetric microarray scanners detect color changes in substrate reactions, often used in simpler assays. Additionally, other systems may include novel technologies that enhance detection capabilities or integrate multiple modalities for comprehensive analysis.</p></p>
