<p><p>Microwave power transmission systems can be classified into two main types: Induction and Magnetic Resonance markets. In the Induction market, power is transferred wirelessly through a magnetic field generated by a transmitter coil and received by a receiver coil. On the other hand, the Magnetic Resonance market involves the use of resonant circuits to enhance the efficiency of power transfer over greater distances. Both types of systems offer promising solutions for wireless power transmission in various applications such as consumer electronics and electric vehicles.</p></p>
