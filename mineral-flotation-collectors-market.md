<p><ul><li>Nonionic Collectors</li><li>Anionic Collectors</li><li>Cationic Collectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral flotation collectors can be categorized into three types based on their ionic charge: nonionic collectors, anionic collectors, and cationic collectors. Nonionic collectors do not have an ionic charge and are typically used for non-metallic minerals. Anionic collectors are negatively charged and are effective for minerals with a positive surface charge. Cationic collectors are positively charged and work well for minerals with a negative surface charge. Each type of collector is selected based on the specific mineral being processed in the flotation process.</p></p>
