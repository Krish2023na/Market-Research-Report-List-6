<p><p>Multi-Assay Screening Systems encompass two primary market types: Low-Throughput Screening and High-Throughput Screening. Low-Throughput Screening involves testing a limited number of samples, allowing for detailed analysis and individualized assessment, often used in exploratory research. In contrast, High-Throughput Screening enables the simultaneous evaluation of thousands of samples, significantly accelerating the screening process, crucial for drug discovery and development. Both approaches support various sectors, including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and academic research, enhancing efficiency in identifying potential candidates for further investigation.</p></p>
