<p><p>The multiaxis CNC machine market consists of various types based on the number of axes they operate on. The 4-axis CNC machine introduces rotation around one axis, enhancing capabilities for intricate designs. Meanwhile, a 5-axis CNC machine provides superior flexibility and precision, enabling comprehensive machining of complex geometries in a single setup.</p></p>
