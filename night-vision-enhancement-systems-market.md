<p><p>Night Vision Enhancement Systems (NVES) are categorized mainly into Near-infrared (NIR) and Far-infrared (FIR) technologies. NIR systems utilize infrared light to enhance low-light images, making them suitable for scenarios where there is some ambient light. FIR systems detect heat emitted by objects, allowing for visibility in complete darkness regardless of light conditions. Both types have unique applications, catering to military, security, and surveillance needs, enhancing situational awareness and operational effectiveness in nighttime environments.</p></p>
