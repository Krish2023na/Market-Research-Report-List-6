<p><ul><li>Combustion Analysis</li><li>Spectral Analysis</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen elemental analyzer market is categorized into three main types: combustion analysis, spectral analysis, and others. Combustion analysis involves burning a sample to convert nitrogen into nitrogen oxides, which are then measured for quantification. Spectral analysis utilizes optical techniques, such as UV-Vis spectroscopy, to assess nitrogen content based on its light absorption properties. The "others" category encompasses alternative methods like chemiluminescence, which measures light emissions during nitrogen reactions, catering to diverse applications in research and industry.</p></p>
