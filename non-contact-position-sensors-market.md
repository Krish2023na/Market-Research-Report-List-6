<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensors</li><li>Rotary Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-contact position sensors market is divided mainly into linear and rotary position sensors. Linear position sensors measure the distance between a sensor and an object along a straight line, often utilizing technologies like inductive, capacitive, or optical sensing. Rotary position sensors, on the other hand, gauge the angular position of an object, employing methods such as magnetic or optical encoders. Both sensor types are essential in various industries, including automation, robotics, and automotive, for precise measurement and control.</p></p>
