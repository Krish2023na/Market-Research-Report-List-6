<p><p>Non-corticosteroid immunomodulators are classified into various types including calcineurin inhibitors, antiproliferative agents, mTOR inhibitors, IMDH inhibitors, and others. Calcineurin inhibitors help prevent rejection in organ transplants by inhibiting immune response. Antiproliferative agents slow down the production of cells in the immune system. mTOR inhibitors target a specific protein involved in the immune response. IMDH inhibitors work by blocking enzymes in the immune system. Other types of non-corticosteroid immunomodulators include various drugs with different mechanisms of action to modulate the immune response.</p></p>
