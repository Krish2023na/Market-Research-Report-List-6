<p><p>The Non-metallic Coating Film Thickness Gauge market can be divided into two main types: Wet Film Side Thickness and Dry Film Side Thickness. Wet Film Side Thickness gauges measure the thickness of coatings while they are still wet, allowing for immediate adjustments during application. In contrast, Dry Film Side Thickness gauges assess the thickness after the coating has cured, ensuring that the final application meets required specifications. Both types are essential for quality control in various industries, including automotive and construction.</p></p>
