<p><p>The non-sugar sweeteners market is categorized into two main types: natural and synthetic. In contrast, synthetic non-sugar sweeteners, such as aspartame, sucralose, and saccharin, are chemically manufactured and often provide intense sweetness with no calories. Both types serve to enhance flavor in food and beverages while catering to dietary preferences and the growing demand for low-calorie alternatives.</p></p>
