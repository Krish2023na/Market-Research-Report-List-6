<p><p>The non-woven glove machine market is segmented into two main types: double-layer and single-layer glove machines. Single-layer glove machines produce gloves with a single layer of material, offering lightweight protection suitable for various tasks. In contrast, double-layer glove machines create gloves with two layers, enhancing durability and providing better barrier protection against contaminants. These machines cater to different applications, with single-layer being ideal for low-risk environments, while double-layer machines are preferred for higher-risk scenarios requiring increased safety.</p></p>
