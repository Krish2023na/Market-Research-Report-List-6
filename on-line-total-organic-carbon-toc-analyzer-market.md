<p><p>The On-line Total Organic Carbon (TOC) Analyzer market includes three primary types: Ultraviolet (UV) Oxidation, UV Persulfate Oxidation, and High-temperature Combustion. UV Oxidation uses ultraviolet light to oxidize organic compounds, allowing for real-time monitoring of TOC levels. UV Persulfate Oxidation employs persulfate oxidation under UV light, effectively breaking down organic materials for measurement. High-temperature Combustion involves combusting samples at elevated temperatures, converting organic carbon to CO2 for accurate quantification. Each method caters to varying application needs and analytical environments.</p></p>
