<p><p>On-Premise Video Conferencing Systems are categorized into Multipoint and Endpoint Systems. Multipoint Systems facilitate video communication among multiple users simultaneously, enabling group meetings and collaborative sessions across various locations. They require robust infrastructure to manage data transmission for numerous participants. Endpoint Systems, on the other hand, focus on a single user or a small group at specific locations, utilizing dedicated devices like cameras and microphones for direct communication. Both types emphasize secure, high-quality video interaction within organizations.</p></p>
