<p><p>Online game platforms can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. This model offers scalability, ease of access, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise platforms require games to be installed directly on local devices, providing better performance and control but often involving higher initial investments and maintenance. Each type caters to different user needs and technological environments.</p></p>
