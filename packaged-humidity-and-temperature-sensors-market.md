<p><p>The packaged humidity and temperature sensors market primarily includes three types: capacitive, resistive, and other variants. </p><p>Capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance caused by humidity levels, offering high accuracy and stability. Resistive sensors rely on the change in resistance within a material as humidity varies, usually being more cost-effective but less precise. Other types encompass a range of technologies, such as thermal or optical sensors, catering to specific applications where unique performance traits are required, ensuring versatility across various industries.</p></p>
