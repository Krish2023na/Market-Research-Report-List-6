<p><strong>The Packaging Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Polypropylene (PP)</li><li>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET)</li><li>Polystyrene (PS) & Expanded Polystyrene (EPS)</li><li>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging resins market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and moisture-resistant, ideal for bags and films. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is strong and rigid, commonly used for containers and bottles. Polypropylene (PP) is durable and heat-resistant, often found in food packaging. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is lightweight and recyclable, widely used for beverage containers. Polystyrene (PS) is versatile for packaging and insulation, while Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and provides excellent cushioning. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is durable, used in cling films and rigid packaging.</p></p>
