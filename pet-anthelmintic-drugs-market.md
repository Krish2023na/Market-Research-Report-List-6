<p><p>Pet anthelmintic drugs market can be categorized into three types: external, internal, and composite. External anthelmintic drugs are applied externally on the pet's skin to control external parasites such as fleas and ticks. Internal anthelmintic drugs are used orally to treat internal parasites like worms in the gastrointestinal tract. Composite anthelmintic drugs contain a combination of external and internal medications. Injection anthelmintic drugs are administered through injections for effective treatment of severe parasite infestations in pets. Each type of anthelmintic drug targets specific parasites and comes in various forms for easy administration.</p></p>
