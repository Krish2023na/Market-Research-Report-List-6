<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Lipids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical lipids can be classified into liquid and solid forms based on their physical properties. Liquid lipids include oils and emulsions, which are commonly used in pharmaceutical formulations for drug delivery due to their ability to solubilize hydrophobic drugs. Solid lipids, such as waxes and fatty acids, are often used in the production of solid dosage forms like tablets and capsules. Both liquid and solid pharmaceutical lipids play crucial roles in enhancing drug solubility, stability, and bioavailability in various pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
