<p><p>Pharmaceutical Restricted Access Barrier Systems encompass two main market types: Closed Type and Open Type. Closed Type systems provide a sealed environment to safeguard against contamination, ensuring products remain sterile during handling and storage. They are commonly used for high-risk pharmaceuticals. In contrast, Open Type systems offer easier access for operators but maintain some level of containment. These systems are suitable for lower-risk medications and facilitate efficient workflow. Both types are essential for maintaining safety and compliance in pharmaceutical handling.</p></p>
