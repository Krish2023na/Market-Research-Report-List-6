<p><strong>The Phenolic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resol Resin</li><li>Novolac Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phenolic resin market is primarily categorized into two main types: resol resin and novolac resin. Resol resin is a thermosetting type characterized by its quick curing properties, making it ideal for applications requiring high strength and durability, such as adhesives, coatings, and molded products. In contrast, novolac resin is a thermosetting polymer that requires a hardener for curing, typically used in applications like electrical insulators and composite materials due to its excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance.</p></p>
