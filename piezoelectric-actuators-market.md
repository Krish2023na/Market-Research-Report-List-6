<p><p>Piezoelectric actuators are specialized devices that convert electrical energy into mechanical motion using piezoelectric materials. The market for these actuators primarily includes stack actuators and stripe actuators. Stack actuators consist of multiple piezoelectric layers arranged vertically, offering high force and precision, making them suitable for applications requiring significant displacement. In contrast, stripe actuators feature a rectangular shape with a lateral movement capability, ideal for applications that require high stability and compactness. Both types cater to diverse industrial and technological needs.</p></p>
