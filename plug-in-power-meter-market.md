<p><p>The plug-in power meter market consists of three main types: analog, digital, and optical plug-in power meters. Analog plug-in power meters provide real-time measurements using needle gauges, offering simplicity and ease of use. Digital plug-in power meters, on the other hand, deliver more precise measurements and often feature displays with additional data analytics. Optical plug-in power meters utilize light-based technology for measurement, suitable for high-frequency applications and providing superior accuracy over a range of conditions. Each type serves different consumer needs and application requirements.</p></p>
