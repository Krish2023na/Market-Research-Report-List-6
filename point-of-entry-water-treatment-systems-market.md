<p><p>Point-of-Entry Water Treatment Systems address various water quality issues through different methods. Water Softening systems reduce hardness by removing minerals like calcium and magnesium. Filtration systems remove impurities and contaminants through physical, chemical, or biological processes. Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems use a semipermeable membrane to purify water by removing ions, molecules, and larger particles. Disinfection systems eliminate harmful microorganisms through chemical or physical methods. Sedimentation systems remove solid particles from water, while distillation systems vaporize and condense water to purify it.</p></p>
