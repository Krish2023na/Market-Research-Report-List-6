<p><p>Polyester Filament Yarn comes in various types such as Partially Oriented Yarn (POY), Polyester Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY), Polyester Drawn Textured Yarn (DTY), and others. POY is pre-oriented and requires further drawing, while FDY is fully oriented during the spinning process. DTY has a crimped appearance and a stretchy texture. Other types in the market include twisted yarn, bright yarn, and color yarn. Each type serves different purposes and are used in various industries such as textiles, home furnishings, and clothing.</p></p>
