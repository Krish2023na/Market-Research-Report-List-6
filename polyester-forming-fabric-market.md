The 4-Shed, 5-Shed, and 8-Shed fabrics are single-layer fabrics, while the 8-Shed Double Layer and 16-Shed Double and A Half Layer fabrics have multiple layers for enhanced performance. SSB Forming Fabric is a specialized type of forming fabric known for its superior stability, strength, and durability in demanding papermaking environments.</p></p>
