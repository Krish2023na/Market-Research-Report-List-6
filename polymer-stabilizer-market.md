<p><p>Polymer stabilizers are additives used to prevent degradation of polymers during processing or use. Antioxidants are added to prevent oxidation, heat stabilizers protect against thermal degradation, and light stabilizers protect against UV degradation. Antioxidants inhibit the formation of free radicals, heat stabilizers prevent thermal degradation by interfering with radical chain reactions, and light stabilizers absorb UV radiation to prevent photooxidation. These stabilizers enhance the stability and durability of polymers, extending their lifespan and maintaining their properties.</p></p>
