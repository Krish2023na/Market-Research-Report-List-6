<p><p>The polymer stabilizing agent market comprises various types, including antioxidants, heat stabilizers, light stabilizers, and others. Antioxidants prevent degradation caused by oxidative processes, extending the lifespan of polymers. Heat stabilizers protect against thermal degradation during processing, while light stabilizers absorb harmful UV rays, preventing discoloration and mechanical property loss. Other agents may include plasticizers and flame retardants, contributing to the overall stability and performance of polymer products across applications in automotive, packaging, and construction industries.</p></p>
