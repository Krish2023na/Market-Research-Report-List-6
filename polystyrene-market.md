<p><strong>The Polystyrene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS)</li><li>High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polystyrene market is primarily categorized into General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is a clear, rigid plastic known for its excellent clarity and low cost, commonly used in packaging, disposable cutlery, and consumer goods. In contrast, HIPS combines polystyrene with rubber to enhance toughness and impact resistance, making it ideal for applications like appliance housings, toys, and automotive parts. Both types are widely utilized due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness in various industries.</p></p>
