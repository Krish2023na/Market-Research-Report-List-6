<p><ul><li>Saline IV Flush Syringe</li><li>Heparin IV Flush Syringe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prefilled IV flush syringe market consists of two primary types: saline IV flush syringes and heparin IV flush syringes. Saline IV flush syringes are commonly used to maintain patency in IV lines and prevent clots by flushing with sterile saline solution. Heparin IV flush syringes, on the other hand, contain heparin, an anticoagulant, which is used to prevent clot formation in patients at risk for thrombosis. Both types improve convenience and ensure accurate dosing in clinical settings.</p></p>
