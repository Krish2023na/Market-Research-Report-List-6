<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Printed Cartons Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paperboard</li><li>Corrugated Board</li><li>Kraft Board</li><li>Coated Paper</li><li>Liquid Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed cartons are available in various types based on the material used. Paperboard cartons are made of solid bleached sulfate (SBS) or solid unbleached sulfate (SUS) paperboard, offering a smooth surface for high-quality printing. Corrugated board cartons have a fluted layer sandwiched between two flat linerboards, providing strength and durability. Kraft board cartons are made from recycled materials, offering a eco-friendly option. Coated paper cartons have a layer of clay or polymer coating for a glossy finish. Liquid board cartons are specially designed for liquid packaging, with a wax coating to prevent leakage.</p></p>
