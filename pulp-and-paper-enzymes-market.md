<p><p>Pulp and paper enzymes play a crucial role in various processes within the industry. The main types of enzymes used in this market are amylase, cellulase, xylanase, and lipase. Amylase enzymes help break down starch molecules into sugar, while cellulase enzymes break down cellulose into simpler sugars. Xylanase enzymes target xylan, a component of plant cell walls, and help in the breakdown of hemicellulose. Lipase enzymes are used for the breakdown of fatty substances in the pulp and paper production process. Other enzymes are also utilized in this market for specific purposes.</p></p>
