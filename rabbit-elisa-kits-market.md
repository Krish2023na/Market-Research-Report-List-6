<p><p>Rabbit ELISA Kits encompass various types, including unconjugated, alkaline phosphatase, biotin, and others. Unconjugated kits utilize rabbit antibodies without any labeling for direct detection. Alkaline phosphatase kits employ an enzyme that catalyzes a reaction, producing a measurable signal. Biotin kits utilize biotin-streptavidin interactions for enhanced sensitivity and specificity in assays. The "Others" category includes alternative detection systems and reagents, offering flexibility for researchers in various applications, including immunology and diagnostics.</p></p>
