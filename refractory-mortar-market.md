<p><p>The refractory mortar market is divided into four main types. Clay refractory mortar is known for its versatility and resistance to thermal shock, making it suitable for various applications. High alumina refractory mortar offers enhanced strength and durability under high temperatures, ideal for industrial furnaces. Silicon refractory mortar provides excellent oxidation resistance, often used in glass and ceramics industries. Magnesia refractory mortar is valued for its ability to withstand harsh environments and is commonly utilized in steelmaking processes.</p></p>
