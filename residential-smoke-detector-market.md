<p><strong>The Residential Smoke Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Residential Smoke Detector</li><li>Dual-Sensor Residential Smoke Detector</li><li>Ionization Residential Smoke Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential smoke detectors come in three main types - photoelectric, dual-sensor, and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light beam to detect smoke particles, making them ideal for detecting smoldering fires. Dual-sensor detectors combine both photoelectric and ionization technology for comprehensive fire detection. Ionization detectors use radioactive particles to detect smoke particles, making them sensitive to fast flaming fires. Each type of detector has its strengths and weaknesses, so consumers should consider their specific needs when choosing a smoke detector for their home.</p></p>
