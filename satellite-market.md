<p><p>Satellite markets can be categorized into four main types: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Geostationary Orbit (GEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and others. LEO satellites, positioned close to Earth, enable low-latency communication and are ideal for internet services. GEO satellites orbit synchronously with the Earth, providing consistent coverage for broadcasting and weather monitoring. MEO satellites, located between LEO and GEO, support navigation systems like GPS. Other market segments may include specialized satellites for scientific research and Earth observation or emerging technologies like hypersonic flight.</p></p>
