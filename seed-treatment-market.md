<p><p>The seed treatment market encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **Chemical seed treatment** involves using synthetic chemicals to protect seeds against pests, diseases, and enhance growth traits. </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Biological seed treatment** utilizes natural organisms, such as beneficial fungi or bacteria, to promote plant health and improve stress resistance.</p><p>3. **Physical seed treatment** includes methods like coating or heat treatment that physically alter seeds to enhance germination and protection without the use of chemicals or biological agents. </p><p>Together, these treatments enhance seed performance and agricultural productivity.</p></p>
