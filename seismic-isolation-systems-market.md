<p><p>Seismic isolation systems are designed to protect structures from earthquake forces by decoupling them from ground motion. Key market types include: </p><p>- **Non-Rubber Bearings (NRB)**: These use materials such as steel to provide flexibility and dissipation. </p><p>- **Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB)**: Incorporate a lead core for energy dissipation, offering enhanced flexibility. </p><p>- **High Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR)**: Utilize rubber with high damping properties to absorb seismic energy more effectively. </p><p>- **Others**: Includes alternative technologies and innovative solutions like friction pendulum systems or sliding bearings, catering to various structural needs.</p></p>
