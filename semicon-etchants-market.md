<p><p>The semiconductors etchants market is categorized into wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents involve liquid solutions that remove material from semiconductor substrates through chemical reactions, offering uniformity and selective etching. Dry etching agents, on the other hand, utilize gases or plasmas to achieve material removal, allowing for precision and complex patterning in microfabrication. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing device performance and integration in advanced technologies.</p></p>
