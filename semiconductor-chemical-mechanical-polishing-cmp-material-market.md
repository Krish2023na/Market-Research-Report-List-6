<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chemical-mechanical Polishing (CMP) Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Pads</li><li>CMP Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Chemical-mechanical Polishing (CMP) material market is primarily divided into CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive materials that provide mechanical support and control the polishing process on semiconductor wafers, ensuring uniform surface finishes. CMP slurries, on the other hand, consist of a mix of abrasives and chemical agents that facilitate the chemical etching and removal of materials during polishing. Together, these components play a vital role in achieving the desired surface precision essential for semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
