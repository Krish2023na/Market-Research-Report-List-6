<p><ul><li>Horizontal Silicone Injection Molding Machine (Himm)</li><li>Vertical Silicone Injection Molding Machine (Vimm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone injection molding machine market is categorized into horizontal (HIMM) and vertical (VIMM) machines. Horizontal silicone injection molding machines are designed for high-volume production, featuring a horizontal clamp and injection unit that facilitates efficient material flow and precision molding. In contrast, vertical silicone injection molding machines utilize a vertical setup, allowing for easy loading of materials and streamlined assembly processes. Each type serves distinct applications based on production requirements, space constraints, and mold design, catering to varying industry needs.</p></p>
