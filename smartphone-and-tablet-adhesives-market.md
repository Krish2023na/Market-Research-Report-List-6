<p><p>The smartphone and tablet adhesives market comprises various types, including urethane, epoxy, and acrylic adhesives, each serving distinct functions. Urethane adhesives are known for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for bonding components exposed to movement. Epoxy adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities and resistance to heat and chemicals, suitable for structural applications. Acrylic adhesives provide rapid bonding and optical clarity, often used for screen assembly. Other adhesive types may include silicone and tape-based solutions, catering to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
