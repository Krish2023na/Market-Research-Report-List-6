<p><strong>The Smartphone Camera Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated Sensor</li><li>Backside-illuminated Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone camera sensor market features two main types: front-illuminated sensors and backside-illuminated sensors. Front-illuminated sensors capture light in a conventional manner but can struggle with low-light performance due to their design. In contrast, backside-illuminated sensors have the photodiodes positioned behind the wiring, allowing more light to reach the sensor, enhancing image quality, especially in low-light situations. As consumer demand for better photography capabilities grows, the preference for backside-illuminated sensors is increasing, driving market dynamics and technological advancements.</p></p>
