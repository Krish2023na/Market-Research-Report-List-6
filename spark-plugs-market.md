<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot spark plugs are designed to operate at a higher temperature to prevent fouling and carbon buildup. They are ideal for driving conditions that involve frequent starts and stops. Cold spark plugs are designed to dissipate heat efficiently to prevent pre-ignition issues and engine knock under high load conditions. They are recommended for high-performance engines or engines that operate at higher speeds for extended periods. Both types cater to specific driving conditions and engine requirements to ensure optimal performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
