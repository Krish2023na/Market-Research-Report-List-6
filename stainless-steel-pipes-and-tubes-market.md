<p><p>Stainless steel pipes and tubes market is divided into two main types - seamless and welded. Seamless pipes and tubes are made from a single piece of metal with no joints, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Welded pipes and tubes are made by welding two pieces of metal together, offering more flexibility in terms of size and shape. Both types have their unique advantages and are widely used in various industries such as oil and gas, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
