<p><p>Stretch film packaging market is divided into two main types: Machine stretch film and Manual stretch film. Machine stretch film is typically used with automated pallet wrapping machines for high volume production and is more cost-effective. Manual stretch film, on the other hand, is used for smaller scale operations where wrapping is done by hand. Both types of stretch film provide protection to goods during transportation and storage by securing and stabilizing palletized loads.</p></p>
