<p><p>The Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) copolymer market is primarily divided into two types: A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA, characterized by its high transparency and excellent mechanical properties, is often used in coatings and adhesives for its strength and flexibility. R-SMA, on the other hand, offers superior thermal stability and is typically utilized in automotive and electrical applications. Both variants are valued for their unique properties, catering to diverse industrial needs including packaging, construction, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
