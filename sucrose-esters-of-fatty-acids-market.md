<p><p>Sucrose esters of fatty acids are categorized based on their Hydrophilic-Lipophilic Balance (HLB) values, influencing their applications in various industries. High HLB (above 9) esters are ideal for emulsifying water-in-oil formulations, commonly used in food and cosmetics. Medium HLB (7-9) esters serve versatile roles, functioning as stabilizers in both aqueous and oily systems. Low HLB (below 6) esters are effective in oil-in-water emulsions, primarily utilized in personal care products and pharmaceuticals, ensuring optimal performance based on product requirements.</p></p>
