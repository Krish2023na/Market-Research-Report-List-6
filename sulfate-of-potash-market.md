<p><p>Sulfate of Potash (SOP) is available in two main market types: Powder SOP and Granular SOP. Powder SOP is a fine, powder-like form of SOP that is easily soluble in water, making it ideal for use in liquid fertilizers and foliar sprays. Granular SOP, on the other hand, is a larger, granular form of SOP that is more suitable for use in traditional soil applications, such as broadcasting or banding. Both forms of SOP provide potassium and sulfur essential for plant growth.</p></p>
