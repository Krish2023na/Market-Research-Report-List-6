<p><p>Thermal insulation packaging market consists of various types such as PUR insulation packaging, metallised insulation packaging, EPS insulation packaging, VIP insulation packaging, and others. PUR insulation packaging utilizes polyurethane foam to provide effective insulation. Metallised insulation packaging uses metalized films to reflect heat and maintain temperature. EPS insulation packaging is made of expanded polystyrene foam. VIP insulation packaging incorporates vacuum insulated panels for superior thermal performance. Other types include products like reflective bubble wrap and insulated boxes that offer different levels of insulation for packaging purposes.</p></p>
