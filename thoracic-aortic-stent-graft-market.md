<p><p>The Thoracic Aortic Stent Graft market comprises two main types based on material: metal and polymer. Metal stent grafts, typically made from stainless steel or cobalt-chromium alloys, offer high durability and strength, making them suitable for complex cases. Conversely, polymer stent grafts, often made from expanded polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) or similar materials, provide flexibility and ease of implantation, with potential for improved biocompatibility. Each type caters to specific patient needs and anatomical considerations in thoracic aortic repair procedures.</p></p>
