<p><p>The Titania ceramic membranes market is divided into two main types: dense membranes and porous membranes. Dense membranes are characterized by their low permeability, offering selective transport of specific ions or molecules, making them ideal for applications like gas separation and ion conduction. Porous membranes, on the other hand, feature a network of tiny pores that allow fluid flow and separation based on size or charge, suitable for filtration and wastewater treatment. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
