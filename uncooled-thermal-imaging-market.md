<p><p>Uncooled thermal imaging technology encompasses various market types based on the wavelength of infrared radiation they detect. Short Wave IR detects wavelengths of 1-3 micrometers, Mid Wave IR detects 3-5 micrometers, Long Wave IR detects 8-14 micrometers, and Far Wave IR detects beyond 14 micrometers. Each market type has its own set of applications and capabilities, ranging from surveillance and security to industrial monitoring and medical imaging. Overall, uncooled thermal imaging serves a wide range of industries and purposes.</p></p>
