<p><p>The varnish remover market comprises three main types: acid, alkaline, and neutral varnish removers. Acid varnish removers use acidic compounds to break down tough coatings, making them effective for heavy-duty applications. Alkaline varnish removers employ alkaline substances, which are gentler and suitable for various surfaces, removing coatings without damaging underlying materials. Neutral varnish removers balance pH levels, providing a mild option that is safe for sensitive surfaces while effectively removing varnish. Each type caters to specific cleaning needs and surface compatibility.</p></p>
