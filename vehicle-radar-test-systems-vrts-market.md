<p><p>Vehicle Radar Test Systems (VRTS) encompass various components designed for the testing and development of automotive radar technologies. Vector Signal Transceivers (VST) facilitate high-fidelity signal generation and analysis, essential for simulating real-world conditions. Variable Delay Generators (VDG) enable precise timing manipulation of signals for evaluating radar performance under diverse scenarios. PXI Controllers serve as the central processing unit, managing test operations and data analysis. Additionally, antennas are crucial for transmitting and receiving radar signals, impacting overall system effectiveness.</p></p>
