<p><p>The vehicle sealing strip market comprises various materials, each serving distinct purposes. Thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), thermoplastic olefins (TPO), and thermoplastic vulcanizates (TPV) are versatile for flexible sealing applications, offering durability and resistance to weathering. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) is commonly used for cost-effective sealing solutions but may lack some elasticity. Ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) provides excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and aging, making it ideal for exterior seals in vehicles. Each material caters to specific performance and application needs in automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
