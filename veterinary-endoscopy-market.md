<p><strong>The Veterinary Endoscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Endoscopy</li><li>Rigid Endoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary endoscopy market encompasses two primary types: flexible and rigid endoscopy. Flexible endoscopy involves the use of long, flexible tubes equipped with cameras and instruments, enabling veterinarians to navigate through the body’s internal cavities with minimal invasiveness. This method is ideal for procedures in areas like the gastrointestinal tract or respiratory system. Rigid endoscopy, on the other hand, utilizes straight, non-flexible instruments, often suited for surgical interventions or visual inspections in specific anatomical locations, such as joints or the ear.</p></p>
