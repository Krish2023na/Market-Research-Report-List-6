<p><p>The viscoelastic market for eye surgery primarily includes sodium hyaluronate and hydroxypropyl methyl cellulose (HPMC). Sodium hyaluronate, a natural polysaccharide, provides lubrication and maintains eye shape during procedures, facilitating smoother surgical operations. Hydroxypropyl methyl cellulose, a synthetic polymer, serves a similar purpose by providing viscosity and stability, enhancing surgeon control. Both materials are essential in ophthalmic surgeries, including cataract operations and corneal transplants, due to their biocompatibility and ability to protect ocular tissues.</p></p>
