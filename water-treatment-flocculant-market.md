<p><p>The water treatment flocculant market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic flocculants. Organic water treatment flocculants, often derived from natural polymers, effectively aggregate suspended particles and are biodegradable, making them environmentally friendly. In contrast, inorganic water treatment flocculants are typically metal salts, such as aluminum sulfate, that work through charge neutralization and are widely used for their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing water clarity and ensuring safe drinking water.</p></p>
