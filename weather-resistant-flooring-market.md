<p><ul><li>Three Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The weather-resistant flooring market features two main types: three-layer and multilayer systems. Three-layer flooring typically consists of a top wear layer, a core layer for stability, and a backing layer for moisture protection, making it suitable for humid environments. Multilayer flooring, on the other hand, incorporates multiple layers for enhanced durability and insulation, often including a decorative layer for aesthetics. Both types are designed to withstand varying weather conditions, ensuring longevity and performance in outdoor and moisture-prone settings.</p></p>
