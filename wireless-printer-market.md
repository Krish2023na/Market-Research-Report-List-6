<p><p>The wireless printer market is primarily divided into two main types: wireless inkjet printers and wireless laser printers. Wireless inkjet printers use ink cartridges to produce high-quality color prints, making them ideal for home users and photo printing. They offer versatile media handling and are generally more affordable. In contrast, wireless laser printers utilize toner for faster, high-volume monochrome printing, suitable for office environments. They excel in producing sharp text documents and are often more cost-effective for large print jobs.</p></p>
